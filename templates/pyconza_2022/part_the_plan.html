The plan

This is what our conference will look like:

  • Live workshops prior to the main event
  • 2 days of talks
  • Sprints held over the weekends
  • Live-streamed presentations, lightning talks and question/answer sessions
  • Chat rooms for attendees to: catch up with fellow attendees, break away into specialized discussion groups (which was very successful in 2020 and 2021), and receive live announcements
  • Live sponsor-interactive events like raffles and competitions
