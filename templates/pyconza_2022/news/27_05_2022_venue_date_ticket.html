You're getting this email because you attended a previous PyConZA event or signed up for our newsletter in the past. Please feel free to unsubscribe if you no longer want to receive these messages.

We have finalized the dates and venue

The PyConZA main event will be held on 13 and 14 October 2022.

The venue is the Premier Splendid Inn in Umhlanga, South Africa.

Ticket sales are open

We have a variety of tickets to choose from. Get yours while they are still at early-bird prices :)

It's important to note that if you pick up an online conference ticket then that will not give you access to the physical event.

The in-person event is an expensive thing to put together and we do pay the venue per head, so if you buy an online-only ticket and you show up at the venue we will unfortunately have to turn you away.

If you want to join us in person, but don't have the funds for the ticket, then you can apply for financial aid. The details are on the tickets page:

${defs.call_to_action(href='https://za.pycon.org/tickets/buy-tickets/',label='Buy tickets')}

Call for speakers

We'll be opening talk submissions next week.

As a community-focused event, we accept, support and greatly appreciate anyone who steps up.

If you are new to public speaking it's worth knowing that PyConZA usually draws a very friendly and supportive crowd. We also have volunteers who are willing to support you as you refine your talk idea, and who can coach you in public speaking.

Register on our site

If you are interested in attending our event, or staying up to date with the latest news, please register on our site.
