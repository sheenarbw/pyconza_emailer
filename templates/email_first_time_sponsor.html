PyConZA, part of the global PyCon initiative and the largest Python software conference in Africa, is a growing community-run conference in its 10th year that will be held on the 7th and 8th of October. We hope you will consider joining and/or sponsoring us.

Why sponsor?

PyConZA is an excellent opportunity to connect and engage with some of the best minds in Africa and abroad. Expected attendance numbers are difficult to gauge at the moment, but given past years successes, we're very positive about attendance numbers this year. Especially as people will not have to travel from around the country and abroad. So we hope you will:

  • Learn and share success cases
  • Connect your brand to open and collaborative free software initiatives etc etc blah blah
  • Train your team
  • Hire cutting-edge scientists, developers and tech-minded people
  • Offer your services and products
  • Get recognition for your brand in South Africa and abroad
